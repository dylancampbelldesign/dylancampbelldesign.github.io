Sounding Type: Urban Soundscape

The brief: Sounding Type required that we choose a sound and create a typographic response that would then be applied to a vinyl cover. I chose to use the sounds of traffic in the city as it was quite unfamiliar to me. The sound was so textured and multi-layered, almost becoming white noise, interrupted by the occasional horn or verbal explosion. From this I used found text from various specimens and created a piece that refelected the urban soundscape.

Typeface: Various old specimen typefaces

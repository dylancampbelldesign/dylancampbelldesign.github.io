Unlimited Limited: Digital Fabrication Revolution

The brief: Digital Fabrication Revolution explores the future of clothing production right out of the home. The Industrial Revolution saw a shift towards mass production of textiles in a factory setting, this has proved to be detremental to humanity and a shift away from this is imagined through a fictional printing device. The home will become the center of clothing production once again through the ability to recycle and design uniqe items of clothing right at the printer.

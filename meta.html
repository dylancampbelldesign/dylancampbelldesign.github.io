Museum of Extra-Terrestrial Anatomy

The brief: META is a museum that has been created from the perspective of an extra terrestrial examining the human race as aliens. It takes a objective view of humanity, focusing on our anatomy, something that the entire human race has in common. The identity relies on a strong use of black and white in order to remain objective and not project a sense of individuality.

Typeface: Akkurat & Akkurat Mono
